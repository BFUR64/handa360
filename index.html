About

Why Handa360 exists

What

Handa360 is a disaster-preparedness web app that generates household-specific guidance using selected risk conditions.

Who

It is designed for families, caregivers, residents, and communities needing easier and more relevant preparedness support.

Why

General advice is often too broad. Different households need different actions depending on location, hazard, and special needs.

Goal

To provide clear, practical, and personalized disaster readiness guidance in a lightweight offline-capable website.
